Expository writing

Newsletters and tutorial videos

I’ve written newsletters for Datamuse, a search engine company that’s been around since the early days of the web. My job involved crawling through CivicSearch, a database that aggregates transcripts of local government meetings from the United States and Canada, to discuss important but below-the-radar issues.

  • Fluoride in drinking water
  • Voter turnout
  • Ranked-choice voting
  • Participatory budgeting
  • Rent control
  • Accessory dwelling units
  • Airbnbs and short-term rentals
  • Immigration
  • Cycling (podcast and YouTube video)
  • Pickleball
  • Harm reduction, the opioid crisis, and safe injection sites
  • Bus rapid transit
  • School absenteeism (and one-pager)

I used Slack to communicate and GitHub to create web versions of the newsletter.

I also scripted, recorded, and edited one-minute tutorial videos for YouTube that demonstrated key features of OneLook, a dictionary–thesaurus–word-finder combo.

  • OneLook subject index
  • OneLook wildcard search patterns
  • OneLook result tabs
