South Hall Literary Magazine

Since October 2023, I’ve maintained the South Hall Literary Magazine website.

  • I use WordPress and some custom HTML, CSS, and PHP.
  • Hosting involves Spaceship and Cloudflare.
  • I manage the submission intake system using SMTP, AppSheet, and Google Apps Script.
  • I've also created a Google Docs add-on (for internal use) to fix common formatting issues while editing submissions, like double spaces and straight quotes.
